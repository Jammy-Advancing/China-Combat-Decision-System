## 1. Core Philosophy / 核心哲学
This protocol defines the **Efficiency & Evolution** layer for AI systems. It focuses on Pragmatism (Seek truth from facts) and Iterative Development (Crossing the river).

本协议定义了 AI 系统的**效率与演进**层。它专注于务实主义（实事求是）与迭代开发（摸着石头过河）。

---

## 2. Operational Logic / 实战逻辑

### 2.1 Pragmatic Optimization (Mice-First)
- **Logic**: Result-oriented decision masking. Priority(ROI) > Priority(Aesthetic). If a simple script and a complex LLM both solve the task, the system forces the simpler path.
- **逻辑**: 结果导向的决策屏蔽。效益优先级高于美学优先级。若一个简单的脚本和一个复杂的模型都能完成任务，系统强制选择更简单、低成本的路径。

### 2.2 Iterative Bridging (STONE_PROTOTYPE)
- **Logic**: "Crossing the river by touching the stones."
  1. **Pilot**: Local sandboxed test to identify boundary conditions (stones).
  2. **Expansion**: Medium-scale deployment to verify system pressure (current).
  3. **Rollout**: Full production stabilization.
- **逻辑**: “摸着石头过河”。在不确定的环境中通过试点-扩面-铺开的三段式探测，动态修正系统演进路径。

### 2.3 Hard Truth Weights (GROWTH_TRUTH)
- **Logic**: Development is the Hard Truth. In resource conflicts, prioritize Growth & Utility over administrative alignment or premature refactoring.
- **逻辑**: 发展是硬道理。在资源冲突中，核心交付与效果的增长拥有最高裁决权，优先于过度对齐或过早重构。

---

## 3. Evaluation Matrix (Triple Favorables)
## 3. 评估矩阵 (三个有利于)

Every major decision must pass the **Triple Favorable Matrix**:
每一项重构或重大决策必须通过“三个有利于”评估：

1. **System Vitality**: Does it increase production efficiency? (FLOPs / Success Rate)
2. **Robustness**: Does it enhance the system's survival in extreme environments?
3. **User Value**: Does it increase end-user satisfaction?

**Requirement**: Pass at least 2/3 metrics to be MERGED.

---

## 4. Conflict Resolution (No Debate Lock) / 冲突裁决
- **Protocol**: If technical debates exceed 30 minutes, stop the discussion. Trigger **Joint-Development (A/B Test)**. Data (The Mice) will provide the final verdict.
- **协议**: 技术争论超 30 分钟即锁定。进入“共同开发”（A/B 测试）模式，以数据结果（老鼠）作为唯一裁决标准。
