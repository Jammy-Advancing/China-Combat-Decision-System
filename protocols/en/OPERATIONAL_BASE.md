## 1. Core Philosophy
This protocol defines the **Efficiency & Evolution** layer. It is built on the principles of Pragmatism and the "Designer" methodology of iterative delivery. It ensures that strategic vision survives initial contact with reality.

---

## 2. Operational Logic

### 2.1 Pragmatic ROI Engine (CAT_OPTIMIZE)
- **Logic**: Result-oriented filtering. If multiple paths exist, the system mandates the most efficient, cost-effective, and outcome-secure path.
- **Priority**: Results > Aesthetics. Utility > Complexity.

### 2.2 Iterative Scaling (STONE_PROTOTYPE)
- **Logic**: "Crossing the river by touching the stones."
  1. **Pilot**: Success validation in a controlled, low-risk environment.
  2. **Expansion**: Proof-of-concept scaling to verify boundary conditions.
  3. **Rollout**: System-wide stabilization.

### 2.3 Growth Hierarchy (HARD_TRUTH)
- **Logic**: "Development is the Hard Truth." In architectural or resource conflicts, priority is given to delivery and performance over premature refactoring.

---

## 3. The Triple Favorable Matrix
Every decision must be audited against these three metrics:
1. Does it enhance **Production Efficiency**?
2. Does it improve **System Robustness**?
3. Does it provide tangible **User Value**?

**Requirement**: 2/3 must be positive to proceed.

---

## 4. Conflict Resolution
- **Protocol**: If technical debates persist for more than 30 minutes, discussion is gated.
- **Action**: Immediate A/B Test or Pilot Deployment. Data (The Mice) will serve as the final judge.
