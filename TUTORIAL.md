# 📖 CCDS Strategy Manual: Command the AI

This guide shows you how to use CCDS to inject high-level strategic thinking into your AI workflows.

---

## 1. How it Works

CCDS exposes its core logic through **MCP Tools**. When an AI Agent (like Claude, Cursor, or ChatGPT) is connected to the CCDS server, it gains access to two "Master-Class" consultants:

-   `strategic_command_audit`: The "Chief Strategist" (Strategic Soul).
-   `operational_base_optimize`: The "Chief Designer" (Operational Base).

---

## 2. Triggering the Strategic Audit

To get the best out of the `strategic_command_audit`, you should provide a broad project context. Use the following prompt patterns:

### Pattern A: Identifying the Principal Contradiction
> "I am building [Project Name]. We have many features planned but resource is tight. Use the `strategic_command_audit` tool to analyze my project context and tell me: What is the **principal contradiction** I need to solve right now?"

### Pattern B: Offensive Strategy
> "We are entering a red-ocean market. Here is our competitor analysis. Trigger the `strategic_command_audit` to design an **asymmetric advantage** strategy. How can we attack where they are weakest?"

---

## 3. Triggering the Operational Optimization

Once you have a strategy, use the `operational_base_optimize` to ensure it can actually be built.

### Pattern C: The "Stone-Prototype" (Mice Theory)
> "We have a strategy to [Goal]. It's risky. Use the `operational_base_optimize` to design a **Pilot-Scale rollout** (摸着石头过河). What is the smallest, safest version of this that proves the ROI?"

---

## 4. Best Practices

-   **Context is King**: The more real-world constraints you provide (budget, competitor data, technical debt), the more cutting the audit will be.
-   **Chain of Command**: Always run a `strategic_command_audit` FIRST to set the direction, then follow up with `operational_base_optimize` to refine the execution plan.
